What Makes This Different

The higher you rise, the harder it is to find people who'll tell you the truth.

Most leadership programs give you frameworks and send you home. We invite you into a room filled with talented peers who understand the terrain, challenge your thinking, and actively support your success.

A game designer's lens on your product

Game designers have spent careers solving the hardest product problem: how do you build something people choose to return to? Habit loops, progression systems, intrinsic motivation — these principles apply directly to your product, your team, and your leadership.

Small, curated & high-trust

Every group is 6–10 people — carefully selected for stage, domain, and fit. Small enough that everyone knows your situation. Diverse enough for genuine perspective. No one selling anything to anyone else.

Weekly rhythm that compounds

Showing up every week keeps your challenges fresh and your thinking sharp. You bring a real decision, get real feedback, and return the following week having acted on it. That cadence builds accountability no quarterly program can replicate.

A coach who tells you the truth

Amy Jo doesn't just facilitate — she challenges. She brings pattern recognition from working with founders across dozens of industries and product categories, and she's not afraid to name the blind spot everyone else has been tiptoeing around.

Who This Is For

Tech Founders & Senior Product Leaders.

We support ambitious leaders with organizational responsibility who are building with AI — tech founders, business owners, and senior product leaders.

You're probably a fit if

  • You're a founder or product leader at a growing company who's facing product leadership challenges
  • You have a technical or product background and it's central to how you think
  • You're spending more time on people, strategy, and stakeholders — and want to lead with product depth, not just manage from a distance
  • You've outgrown the advice of generalist coaches and generic leadership programs
  • You want trusted peers who give honest feedback, not just encouragement

This probably isn't for you if

  • You're pre-product or in the very early idea stage
  • You're looking for a course with curriculum and homework
  • You want networking first and substance second
  • You're not open to having your assumptions challenged

Your Coach

A co-thinker & trusted advisor.

Amy Jo Kim is a game designer and product strategist who has built products played and loved by over 100 million people — including The Sims, Rock Band, Covet Fashion, and Bejeweled 2.

She's worked alongside founders and product teams at Netflix, Disney, Google, Tesla, Replika, and Apollo Neuro, and created the Game Thinking methodology — a framework that distills the best practices of game design into a step-by-step system for building high-retention products.

In the mastermind, Amy Jo isn't a teacher. She's a co-thinker, a provocateur, and a pattern-matcher who has seen what works — and what fails — across hundreds of products and dozens of categories. She'll challenge your assumptions, sharpen your thinking, and occasionally tell you something no one else has been willing to say.
